MORE INFORMATION

Join us for an exhilarating journey through the heart of the City of Angels at the Los Angeles 10K Run on September 7th, 2024! Whether you're a seasoned runner or new to the sport, this event offers the perfect opportunity to experience the vibrant energy of downtown Los Angeles while pushing your limits and achieving your fitness goals.

Starting at 8:00 AM, participants will set off on a scenic route through iconic landmarks and bustling streets, soaking in the sights and sounds of this dynamic city. With the course open until 4:00 PM, runners have ample time to pace themselves and enjoy the urban landscape at their own rhythm.

Priced at just $45, registration for the Los Angeles 10K Run includes access to a memorable running experience in one of the most iconic cities in the world. Don't miss your chance to be a part of this exciting event – sign up today and join us for a day of fitness, fun, and adventure in downtown Los Angeles!
