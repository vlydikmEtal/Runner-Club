The most common questions

We welcome members of all experience levels, from beginners to seasoned runners. Our programs cater to individuals at various stages of their running journey, offering tailored workouts and support to meet diverse needs.

Our programs are designed to accommodate different fitness levels, goals, and interests. Whether you're training for a specific race, looking to improve your overall fitness, or seeking social running opportunities, our coaches can help you find the perfect fit.

While some programs may have age-specific guidelines, many are open to participants of all ages. From our Kids Running Program to specialized programs for older adults, we strive to offer inclusive opportunities for individuals of every age group.

Your safety is our top priority. We adhere to safety protocols and guidelines during all group activities, including ensuring well-lit routes, providing hydration stations, and having trained staff on hand to address any emergencies or concerns.

We encourage anyone with medical conditions or injuries to consult with their healthcare provider before participating in our programs. Our coaches can provide modifications and support to accommodate individual needs and ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.
